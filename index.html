    About the Campaign

    Green Earth is a global initiative dedicated to fighting climate change and deforestation. Since 2005, we have planted over 500,000 trees across 120+ communities in 30+ countries. Our mission is to inspire a greener future by making tree planting easy and accessible for everyone.

    • Reforestation of barren lands.
    • Promoting sustainable living.
    • Creating a greener future.
    • Support for local communities.
